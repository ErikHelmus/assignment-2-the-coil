About

The Coil serves as an indispensable resource and a source of inspiration for aficionados, artists, and enthusiasts alike. With a deep-rooted commitment to the preservation and celebration of traditional tattooing, The Coil provides a window into a world where history, culture, and ink converge. Each issue is meticulously curated to showcase the finest artists, their craft, and the stories that define this extraordinary subculture. In every edition, we aim to transport our readers to a realm where tattoos aren't just ink on skin, but rather, a form of storytelling, cultural preservation, and an unbreakable connection between the past and the present.The Coil is more than a magazine; it's a testament to the enduring legacy of traditional tattooing, a tribute to the artists who keep its flames alive, and a vibrant canvas that invites you to explore, appreciate, and immerse yourself in the captivating world of permanent art. Welcome to The Coil, where tradition meets innovation, and the stories etched in ink continue to unfold.

TCB is where tattoo enthusiasts turn for a taste of true artistry. Renowned for their unwavering commitment to traditional tattooing techniques, this studio embodies the essence of a bygone era, delivering ink that not only tells a story but becomes a part of your story.

At Pearl Harbour Gift Shop, tattoos aren't just ink; they're a voyage into history and a testament to precision. With artists who draw inspiration from the past, each visit here is a journey through time, ensuring your ink is not only a statement but a piece of living heritage.
